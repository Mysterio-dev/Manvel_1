about me

Hi, I'm Manvel, your partner for distinctive branding and efficient business strategies. For over six years, as a passionate entrepreneur from the heart of the Caucasus, I have been helping solo entrepreneurs and SMEs in the DACH region to find their unique brand voice, position themselves effectively in the market and create an unmistakable recognition value.

In a time of crisis and uncertainty, characterized by staff shortages and calls for shorter working hours, small and middle-size businesses in par-ticular are facing major challenges. While young graduates with mini- mal practical experience are already demanding high salaries, small businesses and the self-employed have to focus all the more on their core competencies in order to remain competitive. This is exactly where I come in:

With in-depth experience from my professional sta- tions in Germany and Switzerland, I have been offe- ring high-quality support at fair conditions since 2018. I am no ordinary virtual assistant - I bring a personal approach to every collaboration. There's no superficial blah-blah with me;

I promise you 100% dedication and commitment from our first conversation.

Check, if you need a VPA

You don’t need a virtual assistant if...

  • ... you have no permanent tasks to assign
  • .. you prefer to work only with an assistant from Germany
  • ... you can’t afford any of the hourly rates or service packages listed below
  • ... your tasks require a highly qualified specialist (e.g. programmer, webmaster, software developer, etc.)

You need a virtual assistant if...

  • ... you can offer a workload of at least 2 hours/day
  • ... you need permanent support and not just for a short project
